## Project overview

WeBee is an e-commerce web application for selling bakery products (cakes, pastries, bread) with a **custom cake builder** feature. Customers can customize their orders by selecting option groups such as size, cream type, and toppings before adding items to cart.

The system supports both guest and authenticated purchases. Payment is handled via a static bank transfer QR code. Orders are confirmed via a simulated payment webhook (`POST /webhooks/payment`).

---

## Business purpose

- Allow customers to browse, customize, and order bakery products online.
- Provide an order management dashboard for admins.
- Track customer loyalty points (1 point per 10,000 VNÄ spent) and membership tiers.
- Collect behavioral analytics events for internal reporting.
